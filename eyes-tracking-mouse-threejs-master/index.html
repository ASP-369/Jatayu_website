About Project Jatayu

At Project Jatayu, we are pioneering innovations in drone technology with a focus on societal impact and accessibility. Join us in reimagining how drones can serve humanity.

Why Drones Matter

Our work focuses on creating drones for disaster relief, emergency medicine delivery, and environmental monitoring, showing the broad societal relevance of drones in the modern world.
