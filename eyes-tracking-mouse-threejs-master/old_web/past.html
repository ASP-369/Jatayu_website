Past Drone Projects

Octocopter Sentinel

The eight-rotor drone offers high lift capacity and excellent stability for heavy loads. Its dual batteries provide extended flight time, while the high payload capacity enhances versatility for various applications.

Silver Surfer: The Reconnaissance Plane

The fixed-wing drone features high flight endurance and can carry air-to-air missiles for short-range bombardment. Its high propulsive efficiency and low cost make it an appealing choice for military and defense applications.

Vayu: The Flagship and Rescue Drone

The fixed-wing drone offers high flight stability and extended flight times, equipped with an advanced imaging system for effective data collection. Its communication capabilities enhance operation in complex environments, ensuring optimal performance in surveillance and reconnaissance.

The Balsa structure of Vayu

The laser cutting technique is used to precisely shape a cambered airfoil, which enhances structural stability. This design provides high lift even at low angles of attack, making it efficient for aerodynamic performance.

Eclipse : Hexacopter

This drone features enhanced stability and autonomous flight capabilities, combined with advanced imaging and obstacle avoidance technology. Its modular design allows for easy customization, while offering a high payload capacity for versatile applications.

Project Sampathi

This system is compatible with drones sized between 450 mm to 600 mm, offering 3-axis rotation with slip rings for unrestricted movement. It enables easy determination of drone stability and allows endurance measurement through various maneuvers.
